About

We offer tailored and importantly, independent advice on sensor devices. We specialize in digital data and technology management advice, web design and research services. Sensors have become almost synonymous with horticulture and farming, with growers using a broad range of sensors to better understand the growing environment and to optimise production and postharvest processes. Working and reseaching in horticulture and farming over many years, we can offer real understanding and ensure you get the right advice.
